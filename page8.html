University project / 2021

The aim of this project was to develop our skills in finding new meanings for an object, so that we could rebrand it for a specific purpose and audience. The choice of the object was based on our personal preference, so I decided to use my chessboard as the object I wanted to rebrand.
The USP of my board was that it is a very light and cheap board that can be used wherever you go, so I decided to rebrand it around the idea of travel, which is why my illustration represents different environment in one image, with the chess pieces around it.
Also I made a new packaging that could be used for this board, creating also a new logo.

Medium:
-Adobe Illustrator
-Adobe Photoshop
