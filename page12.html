Boiler Boat

Boiler Boat

This piece was commissioned by Nadir El Haddadi for a techno event happening on the Lake of Garda.
Using a picture of the boat where the event was happening, Nadir asked to have a simple flyer with the names of the DJs and main information: where and when it was happening. 
I also decided to create a simple animation for his social media campaign for the event, giving a more attractive way to see the flyer on digital platforms.

Media used:
Adobe InDesign / Adobe Photoshop / Adobe After Effects
