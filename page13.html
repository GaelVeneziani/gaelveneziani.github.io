About

My name is Gael Veneziani and I'm an Italian/French Illustrator, currently studying a BA(Hons) in Illustration and Visual Media at the University of the Arts London.

Starting my artistic path with crosshatching, in the last few years I have found my interest redirected toward digital art and communicative illustration, both still and animated.

I’ve always taken inspiration from surrealistic art and it’s power, since it does a very effective job of communicating a message to a wide audience, as imagination and reality can be overlapped.

Whenever I'm working on a piece, I try to do so in an innovative and aesthetically pleasing way, using both creative and analytical decisions to represent the subject matter that I previously researched in depth.
